What does macromanager do

Macromanager organizes all of your departments and its employees so you can clearly see who is doing what.

How to get started

STEP 1

Let's start by clicking the SIGNUP button on your home page

STEP 2

Next, ENTER your account information

STEP 3

Now you can SELECT your Organization or CREATE a new Organization

STEP 4

Once you are in your Organization you can SELECT or CREATE a department

If you DO NOT see your current department you can CREATE it

STEP 5

You will see your CURRENT department listed on the TOP of your screen

You can CHANGE your departments name or DELETE it if you wish

STEP 6

Your newly created Task will SHOW above the form

CLICK on TASK INFO so we can ASSIGN the Task to an employee

STEP 7

From the Task Detail page you can view the Department and Empoyees the task belongs to

To ASSIGN the task to an employee, select an employee from the Add Employee Drop Down
